Registration Field Templates

  • {{EventId}} - the unique id for the event
  • {{EventName}} - the party name for the registration
  • {{YourName}} - the contact for the party
  • {{Date}} - the selected date for the party
  • {{StartTime}} - start time for the party
  • {{NumberGuests}} - the number of guests specified for the party
  • {{AverageAge}} - average age specified for the party
  • {{Phone}} - the contact phone number
  • {{GunSize}} - the selected gun size
  • {{UserComments}} - comments specified in the registration
  • {{CompanyName}} - the name of the site
  • {{CompanyAddress}} - the physical address of the site
  • {{CompanyCity}} - the city for the site
  • {{CompanyState}} - the state for the site
  • {{CompanyZipCode}} - the zip code for the site
  • {{CompanyPhone}} - the phone number for the site
  • {{CompanEmail}} - address stored for the site's info address
  • {{CompanyUrl}} - the home page for the site
  • {{VantoraUrl}} - the address to site
